The Power of Words

To create, to be creative, to become the creator of a creation.

Create

Every great endeavor begins with the act of creation. To create is to take an idea, fragile as a whisper, and give it life. It's in the sketch of a groundbreaking invention, the first sentence of a novel, or the initial brushstroke on a canvas. Creation is the courage to start.

Definition: To bring something into existence.

Example: Imagine crafting a melody that moves millions or building an app that simplifies lives.
